You will be given a random number at the start of the game. By clicking on a princess you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by mathcing your total score to the random number given at the beginning. You lose the game if your total goes above the random number. The value of each princess is hidden until clicked on. Each time the game restarts, the value of the princesses are reset. Good Luck!
